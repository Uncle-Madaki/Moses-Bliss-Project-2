Moses Bliss
Moses Bliss is an award winning music artiste, musician, Seasoned minister, songwriter and Young music executive from Nigeria. He has become a leader in the new generation of Music Ministers that are bringing Gospel with unique sounds, grace and deep lyrics to the church community & the rest of the world at large.

He rose to prominence with his Gospel hit "Too Faithful" released in 2019, a song that has gone on to amass over 70 million total streams as well as covers and renditions by fans across the world. Moses Bliss was awarded the Winner of the LIMA Song Of The Year and Best Song of the year by Pastor Chris Oyakhilome for his song You I Live For at the LIMA Awards, Gospel Music's biggest night in Africa.

SPOTLITE NATION
He is the founder of Spotlite Nation. A Gospel Music Record Label, founded in 2023, with over 5 global renowned artiste.

Shades of Bliss
A Fashion Brand owned by Moses Bliss, it's unique styling is based on attires such as Kaftans, Danshiki, Traditional, Casual and Professional wears.

Moses Bliss Foundation
The Moses Bliss Foundation serves as a platform for the less privillege. The Foundation has supported in Schools, Orphanges, Hostipals, Churches and so much more. It's main focus is to support humanity with basic amenities and welfare across Africa and beyond.
